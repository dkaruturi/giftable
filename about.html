ABOUT US

Giftable is your one-stop shop for all your gift basket needs

Welcome to Giftable! We are an e-commerce business that offers a variety of customizable gift baskets. While most gift baskets come prepackaged, we offer a unique service by providing a diversity of low cost options to embellish you gifting experience! Choose from an assortment of rotating options including non perishable foods, socks, coffee, wine, artificial flowers, mugs, candles, comic books, puzzles, stuffed animals, and many more.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

We partner with a host of different businesses to offer you the best collection of gifts. Once you make a Giftable account, you can make a personal gift basket. Each gift basket has a limited weight and space. As you add an item to the cart, you will be notified of how much space remains. Once the gift basket is full, choose your wrapping options and deliver you package! We only hire the most qualified packaging services, so rest assured that you are purchasing a professionally crafted gift basket. INSPIRATION

Giftable was inspired by the desire to give your loved one something really special for a reasonable price. All to often, we have the desire to purchase a large number of gifts in small quantities. However, in practicality, this can be extremely expensive as this is not how items are traditionally sold. The only other option is to buy pre-made gift baskets, creating an impersonal gift-giving experience. To solve this problem, we buy commonly purchased gifts in bulk and give you the product and discount you deserve.
